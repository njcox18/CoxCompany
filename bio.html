Bill has been with the Edward H. Cox Company since 1989. In his current position as President and partner, he is responsible for all financial and administrative duties. Bill also manages the sales and marketing of the company’s hydronic heating and cooling products, as well as the various engineered products. He has extensive practical experience in the design and application of hydronic and radiant heating and cooling systems. Bill has a Bachelor of Science degree in Bioengineering from Syracuse University.
David has been with the Edward H. Cox Company since 1987; he currently fulfills the role of Vice President and is a partner in the company. David specializes in air distribution and ventilating applications where supply and exhaust air systems are critical for the owner’s needs and overall satisfaction. He has extensive training in air distribution and ventilation including displacement ventilation; hospital/clean room environments and laboratory exhaust applications. David holds a Bachelors of Science degree in Economics & Management Science from S.U.N.Y. at Cortland.
Ken joined the Edward H. Cox Company in September 1982. In 2001 Ken became Vice President and a partner in the company. Ken possesses over 40 years of HVAC experience ranging from mechanical contracting, product consultation and currently specializes in the air distribution and ventilating division. Ken has completed advanced training from various manufacturers and assists in product specification selections with our clients. Ken is a graduate of McKinley High School HVAC program and Alfred State College with a degree in heating and air conditioning.
Peter joined the Edward H. Cox Company in 2011 as the Director of Business Development and is responsible for establishing new accounts and partnerships as well as supporting existing customer’s needs. He is actively involved in customer education and implementation of the company’s product offering into the basis of design of new projects. Peter possesses over 25 years of solution selling experience in the building supply industry and has been integral in several initial product launches for energy efficient and engineered building products. Peter holds a Bachelor degree from The University of Buffalo in Environmental Design & Planning.
